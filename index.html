The Wave Temple Community

The Wave Temple is a surfhouse and digital nomad haven just 1 minute walking from the iconic Las Canteras Beach. This website describes the offering of the top floor apartment, with private rooms only. The "Penthouse" space is perfect for both families and digital nomads who want a bit more privacy while still being part of a lively community in the best location in Las Palmas. The Wave Temple project was originally founded by Marc & David (who offer floors 0 & 1), later joined by Antonio (who offers floor 2), and I (Dimas) am the newest member of the team (floor 3).
